Company overview

RSW, formerly known as Raas Scientific Works Private Limited, was founded in 2020 by Mr. R.B. Singh Azad with a vision to revolutionize the surface finishing industry. Recognizing the growing demand for advanced finishing solutions, RSW is committed to setting new standards by delivering cutting-edge, customized, safe, and environmentally friendly technologies that adhere to international benchmarks on a turnkey basis.

Since its inception, RSW has been driven by a mission to provide comprehensive surface finishing solutions that integrate innovative designs with the latest value-engineered technologies. Our approach ensures cost-effective, high-performance results, backed by systematic and continuous customer support to enhance productivity and profitability for our clients.

Quality is the foundation of our work. At RSW, we are dedicated to delivering products and solutions that consistently meet customer expectations without exception. Our commitment to continuous improvement drives us to refine our processes, enhance our products, and invest in the growth of our people, ensuring excellence at every stage.

With a strong presence in India and international markets, RSW operates through multiple service locations, offering complete solutions for the design, manufacturing, and installation of surface finishing plants on a turnkey basis. Our highly skilled management team, with over a decade of expertise in the surface coating industry, leads a dedicated workforce of 100 professionals specializing in production and installation. Their expertise enables us to deliver time-sensitive and precision-engineered solutions that redefine industry standards.

At the core of our operations is a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spanning 4,000 sq. meters, with a constructed area of 2,500 sq. meters. Equipped with advanced machinery and world-class production, testing, and installation capabilities, our facility ensures that every solution we provide is of the highest quality, reliability, and efficiency.

RSW is more than just a technology provider; we are a trusted partner in innovation, dedicated to empowering industries with future-ready solutions that drive efficiency, sustainability, and long-term success.
